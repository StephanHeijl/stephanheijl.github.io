Master Applied Data Science (MADS)

From 2023–2025 I pursued the Master Applied Data Science at the Hogeschool van Arnhem en Nijmegen (HAN). The two-year, 60 EC programme sharpened my expertise in modern data-driven research and production-grade deployment. I graduated cum laude on 26 June 2025.

The curriculum combines rigorous methodology with hands-on, real-world projects. Highlight modules include Predictive Modelling, Data Exploration, Model Deployment and a 16 week Graduation Project, culminating in a public defence.
