Video Artificial Intelligence

TYGERS Magazine

TYGERS Magazine invited me to join their panel on the pilot of their TYGER Talks videos. During this 26 minute video I discuss the state of Artificial Intelligence with 2 other entrepreneurs and a field expert. The discussion is led by presenter Chris Kok.

This video has been produced by TYGERS Magazine in The Hague, at the New World Campus. The content is presented in Dutch.

Panel Members

For this video I was joined by Philip Rakhou, Jurjen Sohne, Tony Nguyen and Chris Kok. We touch on the current state of AI, its potential dangers and benefits, and how and where AI and machine learning is being applied by entrepreneurs.

Discussion Topics

The panel covers the current state of AI, its potential dangers and benefits, and practical applications of AI and machine learning by entrepreneurs in various industries.
