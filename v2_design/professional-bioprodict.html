Reflection

What I learned

Nine years at Bio-Prodict taught me that the best AI systems aren't built in isolation. They emerge from deep collaboration between domain experts and machine learning practitioners. The proteins don't care how clever your architecture is‚Äîthey care whether your predictions are right.

This role shaped my approach to AI: start with the problem, not the solution. Understand the domain deeply. Build systems that work in production, not just in notebooks. And always, always validate against reality.
