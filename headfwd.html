Speaking 2021 Amsterdam

HeadFWD Share&Grow

In 2021 I was invited to present at the HeadFWD Share&Grow event in Amsterdam to give a live presentation on Natural Language Processing.

The content is presented in Dutch. In this video recorded in 2021, I present the latest advances in Large Language Models (LLMs) and how they can be used in practice. This includes a short demo and predictions of where this technology is heading: multimodal models that can see, hear and read, code assistants that will enable faster programming, and integration of AI into our daily lives.

Topics Covered

Large Language Models, GPT architecture, practical NLP applications, multimodal AI, code assistants, and the future of AI integration in everyday tools.
