HeadFWD Share&Grow

In 2021 I was invited to present at the HeadFWD Share&Grow event in Amsterdam to give a live presentation on Natural Language Processing.

  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Videos

The content in presented in Dutch. In this video recorded in 2021 I present the latest advances in Large Language Models (LLMs) and how they can be used in practice. This also includes a short demo and predictions of where this technology is heading: multimodal models that can see, hear and read, code assistants that will enable faster programming and integration of AI into our daily lives.
